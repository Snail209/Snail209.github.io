Simple Stuffed Pepper Recipie


Information

Serves: 2
Duration: 30-60 minutes
Difficulty: Easy





Ingredients

Bell pepper (any colour) 2
White rice 50g
Water 150ml
Grated cheddar xx
Courgette 1
Brown onion 1/2
English mustard 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. In a pot, cover the rice with the water and cook for 15 minutes, or until tender
  2. While the rice is cooking, cut the lid off of the bell peppers, deseed them, and bake in the oven for 15 minutes. The peppers should be baked with the open side up, and the lids with the stem up.
  3. While the peppers are baking, thinly slice the courgette and onion, as well as any extra vegetables you wish to add. Fry them in a pan until nearly done, then remove from the heat.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, drain (if necessarry) and mix with the vegetables, mustard, and cheese.
  5. After the peppers are cooked, spoon the rice mixture into them and top with cheese before replacing the lid of the pepper. Spread any excess mixture around them on the baking tray and also top with cheese.
  6. Finally bake the peppers again for a furher 10 minutes

For a similar recipie, check out BBC Good Food's Stuffed Peppers with Rice.
